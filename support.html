What To Include

When contacting support, include your device model, Android version, app version, what you were trying to do, and any error message you saw. Do not email private health records unless you choose to share them.

Data Deletion

VitalTrack does not currently create online user accounts. You can delete records inside the app, clear app data from Android settings, or uninstall the app. Exported PDF or CSV files must be deleted from the location where you saved or shared them.

Medical Disclaimer

VitalTrack is for personal tracking only and is not a medical device. For medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, contact a qualified healthcare provider.
