Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how VitalTrack handles personal health tracking information inside the app.

Effective date: May 11, 2026

Overview

VitalTrack is for personal tracking only and is not a medical device. It helps users record blood pressure, blood sugar, medicine reminders, medicine logs, profile context, and exported reports.

Information You Enter

The app may store information you add, including blood pressure readings, blood sugar readings, pulse, notes, medicine names, dosage, reminder times, medicine status, age, gender, height, weight, target ranges, and app settings.

How Information Is Used

VitalTrack uses your information to show dashboards, histories, reminders, trends, target status, and PDF or CSV reports. The app does not use your entries for advertising.

Sharing

VitalTrack does not sell your data. Reports are shared only when you choose to save, open, or share an exported PDF or CSV file from your device.

Storage and Security

VitalTrack is designed around local app storage on your device. You should protect your device with a screen lock and avoid sharing exported reports with people you do not trust.

Retention and Deletion

Your tracking records remain in the app until you delete them, clear the app data, or uninstall the app. Exported files remain wherever you save or share them and must be deleted from those locations separately.

Children

VitalTrack is not directed to children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, contact the developer so the issue can be reviewed.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the VitalTrack developer through the support email listed on the Support page. Replace the placeholder support email before publishing the app on Google Play.
